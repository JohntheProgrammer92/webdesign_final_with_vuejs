Cause and effect of smartphones on society

Since 1992 the world has had a boom in mobile computing because of smartphones. Because these devices are so portable and hugely powerful, they enhance our lives. Smartphones enhance the human condition by giving easy access to the internet, allowing for greater organization, and helping us communicate easier.

Having near instant access to the internet helps people do things they may have not been able to do.

Having on the go internet access allows people to find new recipes and diet information anywhere, even in the kitchen. There are many resources online for recipes and guides for cooking. Having a full computer in the kitchen is not practical. So, the portability of the smart phone also comes into play in this scenario. The availability of health-related resources by trusted and knowledgeable providers means that waiting to speak to a professional in person once a week or less, is not the only way to check up on information about diet and food.

Being able to look up reference material for infrequently used skills enables users to stay up to date without formal training. For example, in the computer programming field, when a coding language is created it is completely documented on the source website for use by all who are learning it. Often in the case of vehicles there are lesser known facts that aren’t necessarily professional knowledge that can be found online easily such: intended tire pressure, or oil type.


Also, being able to find out about world events as they happen can keep the user safe, as well as help them avoid those situations Having the internet at your finger tips brings the news to you in a flash. Now a user can hear about an event as it happens and have enough details to react appropriately. Traffic information will literally save your job if you can’t be late, as it gives you a chance to avoid slow downs if you utilize the service.

Smartphones help funnel the vast amount of information we are to process and act upon in a small, neat package.

With so many apps related to productivity available for smart phones it is easier to stay ahead. There are apps for list making with remainders to help even the busiest person with staying ahead of their tasks. Both iPhone and Android phones include AI assistants by default, which in turn offer more options for reminders and quick processing of information.




Business related apps for email, meetings scheduling, and payroll, give a user in a cooperate environment everything needed to manage their work life with minimal interference of their home life. The current Outlook app offers meeting scheduling on the go through invitation emails, making accepting and declining them easier. Being able to reply to and forward emails out of the office, or anywhere, cuts back on turn around time for important communication




Interconnectivity between mobile apps and their PC-based counterparts provide seamless information processing by a user no matter their setting With the arrival of cloud storage apps on smartphones and computers, a user can edit a document in anyway they see fit from nearly all mobile devices and computers. This gives the smartphone an advantage in allowing the user to forgo carrying a laptop daily

There is not a modern form of communication that is not achievable through a smart phone.

With both phone calls and internet calling available, smart phones give flexibility in communication styles There are too many apps to count that allow for voice over internet protocol (VOIP) calls Even if a user doesn’t have access to a standalone phone network, if internet is available through a wifi connection, calls can still be made.


Text-based messaging is easier than ever on a modern smart phone. With full keyboards and autocorrect, typing an email or les formal text message is easy and fast. Voice dictation has also improved tremendously with AI assistants on smart phone


Some smartphones also offer scheduled texts and automatic replies. Being able to schedule a text message will assist those who need to be precise in communicating at certain times. Having automatic replies enabled outside of the office allows users to focus on the reason they are not in the office that day.
